Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Peak Performance Auto ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help the website remember information about your visit, making it easier to navigate and more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

Peak Performance Auto uses a minimal number of cookies to ensure our website functions properly. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality, including navigation and access to secure areas. These cannot be disabled. Session
Supabase Authentication Used to maintain database connections and ensure data is loaded correctly from our vehicle inventory. Session
Stripe Payment Used during the checkout process to securely process payments. Set by our payment processor, Stripe. Session

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses services from the following third parties, which may set their own cookies:

4.1 Stripe (Payment Processing)

  • Purpose: To securely process vehicle deposit payments
  • Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

4.2 Supabase (Database Hosting)

  • Purpose: To store and retrieve vehicle inventory data
  • Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.3 Resend (Email Services)

  • Purpose: To send reservation confirmation emails
  • Privacy Policy: https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. What We Don't Use

Peak Performance Auto does NOT use:

  • Advertising cookies or tracking pixels
  • Social media tracking cookies
  • Analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing or retargeting cookies

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

6.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookie preferences through their settings. You can set your browser to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Prompt you before accepting cookies

6.2 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that if you disable essential cookies, certain features of our website may not function properly, including:

  • Viewing the vehicle inventory
  • Making reservations
  • Processing payments

7. Browser-Specific Instructions

For instructions on how to manage cookies in your specific browser, please visit:

  • Chrome: Cookie settings
  • Firefox: Cookie settings
  • Safari: Cookie settings
  • Edge: Cookie settings
